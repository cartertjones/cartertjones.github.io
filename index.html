About Me


I'm a developer and interactive media designer based in Boise, Idaho, pursuing a bachelor's degree in Game Design, Interactive Media, and Mobile Application Development (GIMM). Since 2022, I've focused on creating educational experiences that use technologies like virtual reality, augmented reality, and interactive storytelling to make learning more engaging and accessible.


My work spans from VR training simulations to educational AR apps, giving me hands-on experience with a wide range of platforms and tools. I enjoy leading projects, solving complex technical challenges, and designing interactions that have a real impact on learners.


Beyond development, I compete as a professional E-Sports player for Boise State's Varsity Overwatch team, an experience that has sharpened my teamwork, communication, and high-performance mindset.
