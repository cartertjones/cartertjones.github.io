About Me

I enjoy managing projects and developing code solutions. I've been fortunate to work on a variety of projects, enabling me to learn a wide range of technologies and programming languages. I'm always eager to improve my skills and contribute to teams I am a part of.

Poor Dan is in a Droop

Digital Comic | May 2023

Technical Stack


Overview

Poor Dan is in a Droop is a semester-long collaboration between 5 people, having 3 main artists and 2 main developers. We were assigned an unproven topic within quantum physics to attempt to convey through interactivity and visuals. This comic portrays the bi-directional flow of time through the life story of Dan and his mental-health struggles.

ABC Stories

Educational iOS AR App Overhaul | May 2024

Technical Stack


Overview

As a GIMM Research Developer, I collaborated with colleagues to overhaul an AR app designed to teach young children letter writing. Initially, the app depended on a website for data management, incurring significant costs to comply with FERPA regulations. Our team redesigned the app to eliminate the need for a third-party host. This transition resulted in our client saving about $1000 per month.

Geoscience Exhibit

3D Exhibit for Android | February 2024

Technical Stack


Overview

As a GIMM Research Developer, I helped develop an android unity experience for an exhibit at the 2024 Personal Structures Exhibition in Venice, Italy. It displays geological drone data gathered by Boise State University's Human-Environment Systems department in an interactive manner. Exhibitors are able to immerse themselves in the environment while learning about invasive plant species in Idaho.

Fish & Game: Hunter Safety VR

VR Simulation | May 2025

Technical Stack


Overview

Fish & Game: Hunter Safety VR is an application developed as supplementary material for hunter's safety classrooms. Our goal was to develop an application that allowed students to get familiar with firearm handling and ethical hunting before being put in real-life scenarios. We collaborated with Fish and Wildlife departments from 13 states on a six-figure budget over the course of 8 months.
